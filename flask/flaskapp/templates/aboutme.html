I'm Jason Emory Andrews, and I am currently a fellow in the Insight Data Science program in New York.

When I'm not doing experimental particle physics or participating in intensive post-doc training fellowships, I sculpt, read, and make beer. I also attempt to write "about me" pages that are whimsical and not at all dry, but that's surprisingly tough!

Have a look at my LinkedIn profile for more information about my technical side. (Interesting tidbit: I did a little CAD work during my PhD program, plus some 3D printing. It was a lot of fun.)
